About Know Botswana

Founded in 2025, Know Botswana is a dynamic platform dedicated to showcasing Botswana’s rich heritage and modern achievements. Our mission is to educate global audiences about Botswana’s Tswana culture, Okavango Delta, and contributions to conservation and diplomacy. From students to travelers, we provide engaging, accurate content to inspire pride and curiosity among Batswana and friends of Botswana worldwide.

“We aim to share Botswana’s story with the world, one connection at a time.” — Founder of Know Botswana, Donovan Ntsima, 2025
